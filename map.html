The GHIA mapping tool collects information about your use of green space in Greater Manchester.

Collecting your views

Once you have located a place you are interested click on that place and tell us your thoughts about it.

Finding your places

You can zoom in to any area of the map or search for a place you know.

Using your responses

We will use what you tell us to develop a better understanding of how you use these places and what benefits they provide to you.
